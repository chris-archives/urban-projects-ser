About Us

URBAN PROJECTS SPORTS EQUIPMENT REDISTRIBUTION

We are a nonprofit that helps underserved Philadelphia students by giving them gently used sports gear. We strive to provide all kids with the chance to play and stay active, promoting healthier lifestyles and a stronger community.

Our Focus

REDEFINING THE SPORTS LANDSCAPE

By recycling gently used equipment, we aim to give a new home to sports gear, and reach students in of physical activity across the Philadelphia School District and greater Philadelphia

Our Impact

We Are Happy to Serve the Following Schools:

Since our founding in June 2022, we have served over 20 schools, with a team consisting of more than 20 high school students to bring sports equipment to underserved schools. If your school is in need of any specific equipments from these categories, feel free to reach out to us!

  • Basketball Equipment
  • Soccer Equipment
  • Baseball Equipment
  • Recess Equipment
  • Tennis Equipment
  • Yoga Equipment
Our Mission

Empowering Youth

Through our commitment to making sports more accessible to children in need, we hope to promote participation in fitness and enjoyment of physical activity in areas that otherwise may be limited.
